# Birdfolk

## Level 0

The birdfolk are avian people of every shape and bright plumage, endlessly sociable, and
famous for collecting and carrying true stories.

## Level 1

Birdfolk are avian people of bright plumage and relentless sociability, famous for
collecting true stories. A story carried by birdfolk is expected to be accurate, though not
always comfortable.

They can be charming, intrusive, generous, and impossible to bore. A birdfolk speaker may
value the exact shape of an event more than whether the truth flatters anyone.
