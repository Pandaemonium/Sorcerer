# Crystal Magic

## Level 0

Crystal magic is Stalnaz's signature art: light and song coaxed through cut stone.

## Level 1

Unlike most traditions, crystal magic has a real material edge: a crystal stores magical
energy, so one hand can slowly charge it and another draw it out later. It is why Stalnaz
could build a civilization on stone.
