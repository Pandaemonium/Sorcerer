# Parn

## Level 0

The Parn are tattooed nomads of the desert, traveling in caravans with no king and a deep
love of music.

## Level 1

The Parn are tattooed desert caravan folk with no king, a deep love of music, and long
memories carried in routes, songs, and skin. A tattoo may mark kinship, grief, bargain, or
the shape of a journey.

Parn speakers may treat place as movement rather than possession. Roads, wells, songs, and
safe nights matter more than walls.
