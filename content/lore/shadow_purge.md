# Shadow Purge

## Level 1

The Shadow Purge is the horror every schoolchild knows: a cult of shadow sorcerers loosed
body-jumping shadow spirits through a great Vigovian city, turning the possessed against
their own families. The Empire has justified the charter by it ever since.
