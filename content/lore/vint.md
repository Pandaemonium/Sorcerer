# Vint

## Level 0

Vint is a gossipy republic famed for its woven charms and tapestries and its ever-churning,
rumor-driven politics.

## Level 1

Vint is a republic of councils, gossip, woven charms, and politics that move like a rumor
through a market. Its tapestries are trade goods, histories, insults, and sometimes spells.

Vintan speakers often know who is feuding with whom, or at least claim to. A useful Vintan
fact may arrive as a story about someone else's aunt.
